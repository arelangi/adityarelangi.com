2015 - Looking Back - Year in Review

17 January 2016

Lot of plans were made for 2015. Some worked out, many didn't. Let's take a look at it.

Negatives

Books

I wanted to read fewer, better books and write about them. I've read more than I planned on but never wrote enough reviews for them.

Fitness

This is something I wanted to improve on and for a while I did good and then fell back into old habits.

Writing

The implementation of this goal has been terrible on the whole.

Positives

Burning Man

I went to Burning Man in 2015. Great time. Met amazing people. Made a good friend. Explored new horizons. A great positive.

Travel

I travelled quite a bit. Saw some amazing places. I plan on continuing to do this further.

New Job

I changed jobs. Left the old startup I was at and started working for a bank. It's good so far.
